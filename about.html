About Me

My Life Journey

I was born on February 24, 2003, in Cavite, Philippines, where I grew up in a close-knit family of five. Being the eldest, with twin younger brothers, taught me responsibility early on and shaped how I value patience, teamwork, and support.

My formative years were spent in schools that emphasized discipline and growth, where I developed both academic foundations and personal values. These experiences helped me build resilience, curiosity, and a steady drive to improve—traits that continue to guide me today.

At present, I am a Computer Science student specializing in Software Technology, balancing academics with creative and physical pursuits such as music and sports. I strive to live with intention—constantly learning, refining my craft, and working toward becoming both a capable developer and a grounded individual.
