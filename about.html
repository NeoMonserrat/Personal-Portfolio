My Life Journey

I was born on February 24, 2003, in Cavite, Philippines, and grew up in a close-knit family of five. As the eldest, with twin younger brothers, I learned responsibility early and developed a deep appreciation for patience, teamwork, and support.

Over the years, my education emphasized discipline and steady growth, helping me build resilience and curiosity. Today, I am a Computer Science student specializing in Software Technology, balancing academics with creative and physical pursuits such as music and sports.
